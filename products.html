Before You Visit

Imported, live and seasonal stock can change quickly.

Is every item available every day?

No. This is the complete range Crab Talk is focusing on, but exact stock depends on current arrivals. Sauce and mantou are available only when in stock.

Can I arrange a larger quantity?

Yes. Contact us early with your required quantity and collection date so we can confirm or arrange supply.

Can seafood be prepared?

Suitable seafood can be washed, cleaned, cut and packed. Confirm your preferred preparation when ordering.
