What Are Vannamei Prawns?

Vannamei prawns are also known as Pacific white shrimp or whiteleg shrimp. They are popular because the flesh is naturally sweet, firm and versatile enough for steaming, boiling, grilling, hotpot, har lok and noodle dishes.

Common nameVannamei / whiteleg prawn
TextureFirm and bouncy when cooked correctly
FlavourClean natural sweetness

What Does “About 20 Prawns Per Kg” Mean?

Prawn sizing is often described by the approximate number of prawns in one kilogram. A lower count generally means larger individual prawns. At around 20 prawns per kg, each prawn has a substantial visual size for sharing dishes and premium home meals.

The actual count can vary slightly because live prawns are not identical in size.
