A real market seafood experience

Commonwealth Crescent Market gives customers a chance to visit physically, see live seafood directly and ask questions before buying.

What you may find at Crab Talk

Depending on daily arrivals, Crab Talk may carry live crabs, Sri Lanka Yellow Roe Crabs, lobsters, Manila clams, Soon Hock and other seasonal seafood.

Home cooking or nearby cooking

Customers can buy seafood for home cooking. Selected items may also be arranged for nearby hawker-style cooking depending on availability.

Why WhatsApp helps

Daily stock changes quickly. WhatsApp helps customers check availability, sizes and reservation options before coming down.

Good for seafood lovers

Crab Talk is suitable for customers who enjoy choosing seafood physically rather than ordering from a purely online shop.

Where is Crab Talk located?

Yes. Customers can visit the stall and choose seafood from the tanks.
