Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book the Crab Talk x Mod Keaw live crab dine-in?

Check crab availability with Crab Talk first. Then WhatsApp Mod Keaw at 8088 6243 to reserve your table and quote CTMK.

Which WhatsApp number should I use?

Use Crab Talk WhatsApp 9839 8671 for seafood stock and crab availability. Use Mod Keaw reservation WhatsApp 8088 6243 for table booking and dine-in timing.

Is Mod Keaw replacing Crab Talk at Commonwealth Crescent?

No. Crab Talk continues to operate as a live seafood stall at Commonwealth Crescent Market. Mod Keaw is an additional dine-in option for selected crab sets.

What cooking styles are available?

Available styles may include chilli crab, black pepper crab, crab bee hoon and glass noodle crab. Please confirm available styles when booking.

Can I still buy live crab from Crab Talk for home cooking?

Yes. Customers who prefer home cooking can still buy live seafood directly from Crab Talk at Commonwealth Crescent.
