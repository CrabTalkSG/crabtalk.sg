Common Questions

Simple answers for customers before they reserve.

Does this replace Fai Kee?

No. Mod Keaw is an additional dine-in option at 243 Cantonment Road. Fai Kee and other Commonwealth cooking options remain separate from this collaboration.

Who handles the cooking and dine-in experience?

Mod Keaw handles the cooking, service, payment and dine-in experience. Crab Talk supplies the live crabs for this collaboration.

How do I get free mantou?

Quote promo code CTMK when reserving with Mod Keaw, or show the Crab Talk x Mod Keaw promo video, to enjoy free mantou with your crab order.

Are the prices permanent?

These are launch specials, limited sets only and while stocks last. Prices, sizes and cooking styles may change based on availability and kitchen confirmation.
