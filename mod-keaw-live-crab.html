Positioning the Collaboration Clearly

Crab Talk remains the main live seafood stall at Commonwealth Crescent Market. Mod Keaw is an additional dine-in route for customers who want a sit-down meal with selected crab sets.

This makes the customer journey clearer: home cooks can continue to buy from the stall, while customers who prefer restaurant-style dining can consider the Mod Keaw collaboration.

Availability, sets and cooking styles should be confirmed before visiting. This keeps the experience controlled, premium and practical.

Best Customers for Crab Talk x Mod Keaw

The collaboration works best when customers want a convenient dine-in crab experience rather than preparing seafood at home.

  • Good for groups who want a sit-down crab meal
  • Useful for customers who do not want to cook at home

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers before visiting or reserving with Crab Talk.

Is Mod Keaw replacing Crab Talk at Commonwealth Crescent?

No. The Mod Keaw collaboration is an additional dine-in option. Crab Talk continues to operate as a live seafood stall at Commonwealth Crescent Market.

What cooking styles are available?

Available styles may include chilli crab, black pepper crab, crab bee hoon and glass noodle crab, subject to arrangement.

Should I reserve before visiting Mod Keaw?

Yes. Reservation is recommended because crab sets and seating need to be confirmed.

What should I quote when booking?

Quote CTMK when checking the Crab Talk x Mod Keaw collaboration details.
