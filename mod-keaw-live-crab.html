Best For These Customers

This dine-in option is useful for customers who want Crab Talk-style live crab but prefer a restaurant setting. It is especially suitable for small groups, drinks sessions, casual celebrations and customers who do not want to cook or clean up at home.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reserve the Crab Talk x Mod Keaw crab promo?

Is Mod Keaw the same as buying live seafood from Crab Talk?

No. Mod Keaw is an additional dine-in option. Customers can still visit Crab Talk at Commonwealth Crescent Market to choose live seafood for home cooking.

Where is Mod Keaw located?
