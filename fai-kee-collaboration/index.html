Why choose Crab Talk?

Crab Talk is a physical live seafood stall at Commonwealth Crescent Market. Customers visit us to see the seafood directly, choose from the tanks, and ask for practical recommendations based on freshness, size, cooking method and budget.

Our seafood selection changes by day. That is why we recommend checking with us by WhatsApp before visiting, especially for premium items, seasonal seafood, roe crabs and larger lobster or crab sizes.

Freshness, service and honest recommendations

Customers often mention our friendly service, clear advice and willingness to recommend the best catch of the day. We focus on practical seafood for home cooking, family meals and special occasions, with preparation assistance where suitable.

Good for home cooking or nearby cooking options

You can buy seafood for home cooking, or ask us about nearby cooking arrangements when available. Cooking style and timing may depend on the item, effort required and partner stall availability.

Frequently asked questions

Can I reserve before visiting?

Yes. WhatsApp Crab Talk to check daily availability and reserve when suitable.

Are prices fixed?

Seafood prices and sizes can change by arrival and stock. Please check before visiting.

Can seafood be cleaned and packed?

Yes. We can help clean, cut and pack suitable seafood for home cooking.
