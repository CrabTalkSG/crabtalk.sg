How to Choose Live Seafood in Singapore

For live seafood, freshness is best judged by condition, tank care, movement, shell quality, smell and how suitable the seafood is for your cooking style. At Crab Talk, customers can check live seafood in person before buying, then request cleaning and packing for home cooking.

For crabs, compare weight, firmness and activity. For lobsters, check shell condition and movement. For clams, look for clean shells and fresh sea smell. For fish, check clear eyes, firmness and active swimming where possible.
