How Crab Talk Helps You Plan a Better Seafood Meal

A live seafood purchase is different from buying a fixed frozen pack. Size, weight, shell condition, meat yield and daily arrival quality can change. That is why Crab Talk keeps the buying process simple: check what is available, choose what fits your meal, then reserve before coming down.

For home cooks, this means less guesswork. You can decide whether the meal should be crab-focused, lobster-focused, fish-focused or a mixed seafood table. For larger gatherings, WhatsApp ahead so the stall can advise on portions and availability.

Our aim is simple: real tanks, clear recommendations, practical seafood advice and a premium but approachable market experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers before visiting or reserving with Crab Talk.

Where can I buy live seafood in Singapore?

Crab Talk is located at 31 Commonwealth Crescent Market #01-41/42/43 and offers live crabs, lobsters, clams, oysters, Soon Hock and seasonal seafood subject to daily arrivals.

Should I WhatsApp before visiting?

Yes. Availability, sizes and premium seafood items change daily, so WhatsApp before visiting if you need a specific seafood item.

Can seafood be cleaned and packed for home cooking?

Cleaning, cutting and packing may be available depending on the seafood selected and stall operations. Ask when reserving or at the stall.

What seafood is best for a premium home meal?

Popular combinations include live crab with Manila clams, yellow roe crab with oysters, and lobster with Soon Hock for a fuller premium seafood table.
