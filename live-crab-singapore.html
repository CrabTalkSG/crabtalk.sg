What cooking styles are suitable for live mud crab?

Live mud crab is commonly used for chilli crab, black pepper crab, ginger and scallion crab, steaming and other home-cooked crab dishes. Tell Crab Talk your cooking style before reserving so the team can suggest a suitable size.

What is the difference between live mud crab and yellow roe crab?

Live mud crab is usually chosen for meaty crab dishes such as chilli crab and black pepper crab. Yellow roe crab is a more premium, roe-focused option and is better for customers who specifically want roe texture and flavour.
