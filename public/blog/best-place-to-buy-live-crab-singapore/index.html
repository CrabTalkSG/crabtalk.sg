Why buying live crab matters

Live crab gives customers a clearer view of freshness, movement and condition before purchase. Instead of buying blindly, you can compare sizes and ask which crab suits your meal.

Live seafood tank vs supermarket crab

Supermarket crabs may be convenient, but live seafood tanks offer a more transparent buying experience. You can see whether the crab is active and choose based on the day’s available stock.

What to look for when buying live crab

Look beyond weight. Ask about shell condition, meat quality, suitable cooking style and whether the crab is better for steaming, chilli crab, black pepper crab or bee hoon.

Why Crab Talk is suitable for live crab buyers

Crab Talk is a physical live seafood stall at Commonwealth Crescent Market. Customers can choose directly from tanks and WhatsApp ahead to check daily availability.

Best time to check availability

Availability changes daily, especially during weekends and festive periods. If you need a specific size or quantity, check with Crab Talk before coming down.
