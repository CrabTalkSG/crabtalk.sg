Commonwealth Crescent Market is where customers can visit Crab Talk to see live seafood directly from the tanks before buying.

Depending on daily arrivals, Crab Talk may carry live crabs, Sri Lanka Yellow Roe Crabs, lobsters, Manila clams and Soon Hock.

Customers can buy seafood for home cooking. Selected items may also be arranged for nearby hawker-style cooking depending on availability.
