Live Lobster vs Frozen Lobster: What Is the Difference?

Compare live lobster and frozen lobster so you can decide which is better for texture, sweetness, cooking style and occasion.

4 min readUpdated May 2026Crab Talk Guide

Quick Takeaways

✓Live lobster usually gives a fresher texture and cleaner sweetness.
✓Frozen lobster is convenient but can lose some texture.
✓Choose live lobster for premium occasions and sharing dishes.

Freshness and texture

Live lobster is often preferred because the texture can be firmer and the sweetness cleaner when cooked properly. Frozen lobster can still be useful, but texture may vary depending on freezing and thawing.

Cooking flexibility

Live lobster works well for steaming, grilling, lobster yee mee and lobster bee hoon. The natural flavour can carry the dish more strongly.

Convenience vs experience

Frozen lobster is convenient because it can be stored longer. Live lobster is more suitable when you want a premium seafood experience and a stronger visual impact.

When to choose live lobster

Choose live lobster for celebrations, family meals and dishes where lobster is the main attraction.
