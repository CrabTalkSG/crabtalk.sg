How to Choose Live Mud Crab in Singapore

Learn how to choose live mud crabs based on size, shell condition, weight, meat quality and cooking style.

Quick Takeaways

✓Choose crab based on cooking style and number of diners.
✓Weight alone does not guarantee quality.
✓Ask for recommendations when buying from live tanks.

Start with how you want to cook it

A good crab choice depends on the dish. Chilli crab, black pepper crab, steamed crab and crab bee hoon may all suit different sizes and meat profiles.

Look beyond weight

Many customers focus only on kilograms, but a good crab also depends on freshness, shell condition and meat quality. A large crab is not useful if the condition is poor.

Shell condition matters

A firm shell can be a useful sign of maturity and meat condition. Weak or soft shells may affect the eating experience depending on the crab.

Ask what is best today

At Crab Talk, daily arrivals change. The best crab today may not be the same as yesterday, so it is better to ask based on your budget and dish.

Reserve during peak periods

Weekend and festive demand can be strong. WhatsApp ahead if you need a specific size or quantity.

How do I know if a mud crab is good?

Look at freshness, shell condition, firmness and suitability for your cooking style.

Is heavier crab always better?

No. Weight matters, but quality and meat condition are also important.
