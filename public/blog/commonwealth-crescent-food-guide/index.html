Quick Takeaways

✓Commonwealth Crescent Market offers a local wet market and hawker food experience.
✓Crab Talk adds a live seafood tank selection experience.

A neighbourhood food destination

Commonwealth Crescent Market has a local, practical food culture. It is not a tourist-style seafood restaurant area, which makes it appealing for customers who enjoy authentic market experiences.

Live seafood at Crab Talk

Crab Talk gives customers the chance to see live seafood in tanks before buying. This includes live crabs, roe crabs, lobsters, clams, fish and seasonal items depending on daily arrivals.

Seafood plus hawker-style cooking

Selected seafood may be arranged for nearby cooking depending on availability. This creates a unique market-to-table experience.

Why visit in person

Visiting in person allows you to compare seafood, ask questions and choose based on today’s actual stock.

Check before travelling

Because seafood supply changes daily, WhatsApp Crab Talk before visiting for current availability.
