Best Seafood for Home Cooking in Singapore

A practical guide to choosing seafood for home cooking in Singapore, from live crabs and lobsters to clams and premium fish.

Quick Takeaways

✓Choose seafood based on cooking confidence and preparation time.
✓Clams and fish are beginner-friendly for home cooks.
✓Live crab and lobster are great for special family meals.

Start with simple seafood

For home cooking, choose seafood that suits your kitchen setup and confidence level. Manila clams, fish and prawns are usually easier than large crabs or lobsters.

Best seafood for steaming

Soon Hock, grouper, lobster and roe crab can work well with steaming when the seafood is fresh. Steaming preserves natural sweetness and keeps the dish clean.

Best seafood for sharing

Live crabs and lobsters are good for family meals and special occasions. They feel more premium and create a stronger dining experience at home.

Best seafood for quick cooking

Clams are one of the easiest seafood items for quick meals. They can be steamed, cooked with garlic, added to soup or stir-fried with sauces.
