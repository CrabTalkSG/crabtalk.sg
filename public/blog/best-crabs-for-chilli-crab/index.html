Best Crabs for Chilli Crab

Learn which crabs are best for chilli crab, why mud crab is popular and when roe crab may not be the best choice.

Quick Takeaways

✓Live mud crab is a strong choice for chilli crab.
✓Meat quality matters because chilli crab is a sauce-heavy dish.
✓Premium Yellow Roe Crab is usually better cold or grilled.

Why mud crab is popular for chilli crab

Mud crab has strong shell presence, satisfying meat and good sauce compatibility. It works well with thick chilli crab sauce.

Meat crab vs roe crab

For chilli crab, meat crab is often more suitable because the sauce focuses on meat and shell flavour. Premium Yellow Roe Crab may be better enjoyed cold or grilled.

Size matters

The best size depends on diners and budget. Larger crabs can look impressive, while smaller pieces may be easier to share.

Freshness affects the dish

Fresh crab gives the sauce better sweetness and reduces muddy or stale flavours.
