Yellow Roe Crab vs Meat Crab: Which Should You Choose?

Compare Yellow Roe Crab and meat crab so you can choose the right crab for your meal, cooking style and budget.

4 min readUpdated May 2026Crab Talk Guide

Quick Takeaways

✓Yellow Roe Crab is for roe lovers who want creamy richness.
✓Meat crab is better for customers who want more crab meat.
✓Cooking style should guide your crab choice.

The main difference

Yellow Roe Crab is valued for its roe, while meat crab is mainly chosen for the amount and firmness of crab meat. Both can be premium, but they satisfy different cravings.

When to choose Yellow Roe Crab

Choose Yellow Roe Crab when you want creamy roe, rich flavour and a premium eating experience. It is best enjoyed simply, especially as Cold Crab or Grilled Crab.

When to choose meat crab

Choose meat crab when the dish depends on flesh, sauce and sharing. Meat crabs are often suitable for chilli crab, black pepper crab and crab bee hoon.

Price differences

Yellow Roe Crab can cost more because roe quality and supply are limited. Meat crab pricing may depend more heavily on size, weight and availability.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Yellow Roe Crab better than meat crab?

Not always. Yellow Roe Crab is better for roe lovers, while meat crab is better for customers who want more flesh.

Which crab is best for chilli crab?

Meat crab or live mud crab is usually more suitable for chilli crab.

Which crab is best for cold crab?

Premium Yellow Roe Crab is a strong choice for Cold Crab.
