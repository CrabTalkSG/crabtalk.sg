Best Way to Eat Yellow Roe Crab in Singapore

The two best ways to enjoy premium Yellow Roe Crab in Singapore: Cold Crab and Grilled Crab.

Keep It Simple

Premium Yellow Roe Crab is best enjoyed with simple preparation. Heavy sauces can cover the natural sweetness of the crab meat and the richness of the roe.

Cold Yellow Roe Crab

Cold Crab is steamed first, then chilled. This method gives the roe a creamy texture and lets the natural sweetness of the crab meat stand out.

Grilled Yellow Roe Crab

Grilled Yellow Roe Crab adds a light smoky aroma while keeping the roe rich and satisfying.

Avoid Overpowering Sauces

For premium roe crab, Cold Crab and Grilled Crab are more premium choices than heavy sauces.
