Alaska King Crab vs Mud Crab

Mud crab and Alaska King Crab are very different eating experiences. Mud crab is commonly used for Singapore-style dishes such as chilli crab and black pepper crab, while Alaska King Crab is often enjoyed for its large leg meat and premium texture.

If you want local-style crab dishes, live mud crab may be more suitable. If you want a premium seafood centrepiece, Alaska King Crab is a strong choice when available.

  • Choose Alaska King Crab for premium leg meat
  • King crab is usually more limited
  • Ask about availability before visiting
