Simple Cooking Ideas for Kelong Sotong

Quick favourite: boil the squid briefly in salt water, then serve it simply so the natural sweetness comes through. This is one of the cleanest and easiest ways to enjoy it.

Weeknight option: slice and stir-fry with ginger, spring onion and a light soy-based sauce for a fast dinner with rice.

Spicier choice: use it for sambal sotong if you want something richer and more bold.

Sharing dish: grill or pan-sear lightly and finish with butter, garlic and a squeeze of lemon.
