Live Mud Crab vs Sri Lanka Yellow Roe Crab

Mud crab is usually the more practical choice for chilli crab, black pepper crab and other saucy dishes. It is often chosen for group meals and home-cooked crab nights.

Sri Lanka yellow roe crab is more premium and roe-focused. It is usually chosen by customers who specifically enjoy roe and want a richer crab experience.

Both options are useful, but they serve different customer needs. WhatsApp Crab Talk before visiting so we can advise based on actual stock and your preferred dish.
