How a visit to Crab Talk works

Most customers visit for one simple reason: they want to see the seafood before buying. From there, we help with practical suggestions based on size, freshness, cooking style and today’s stock.

01

Choose live seafood

See what is available in the tanks before buying. Stock changes daily depending on supply.

02

Ask about size and cooking style

Tell us how you plan to cook. We can suggest suitable sizes and seafood for steaming, bee hoon, yee mee or crab dishes.

03

Clean, cut and pack

Where suitable, we can help prepare the seafood so it is easier to bring home and cook.

04

Cook at home or nearby

Some customers cook at home. For selected items, nearby cooking may be available, depending on timing and stall arrangements.
