Yellow Roe Crab vs Meat Crab

Meat crab is usually chosen for chunky meat and saucy dishes like chilli crab or black pepper crab. Yellow roe crab is chosen for roe richness and a more indulgent eating experience. If your guests love roe, yellow roe crab is usually the more memorable choice.

If your guests prefer big crab meat and heavy sauces, live mud crabs may be more suitable.
