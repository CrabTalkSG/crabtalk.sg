Yellow Roe Crab vs Normal Meat Crab

Normal meat crab is usually chosen for saucy dishes, sharing and value. Yellow roe crab is more niche and premium. It is better for customers who specifically enjoy roe and want a more special crab experience.

If your group mainly wants chilli crab or black pepper crab, live mud crab may be more practical. If your group wants something richer and more memorable, Sri Lanka yellow roe crab can be the better choice.
