About Live Seafood Singapore

Crab Talk is a physical live seafood stall at Commonwealth Crescent where customers can see and choose seafood directly from the tanks. The selection changes with daily arrivals and may include live crabs, roe crabs, lobsters, oysters, Manila clams and live fish.

For the best experience, visit the stall to view the seafood in person. The team can recommend suitable options based on freshness, size, intended cooking style and daily supply.

  • Physical live seafood stall at Commonwealth Crescent
  • WhatsApp for today’s availability and reservations
  • Fresh seafood selected from market tanks
  • Cleaning, cutting and packing available for home cooking
Can I reserve before visiting?

Yes. WhatsApp Crab Talk to check today’s arrivals and availability before heading down.

Is Crab Talk an online seafood shop?

No. Crab Talk is primarily a physical live seafood stall. WhatsApp is used for availability checks and reservation enquiries.
