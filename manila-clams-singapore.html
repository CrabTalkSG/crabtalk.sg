Why Manila Clams Are Popular

Good Manila clams should taste naturally sweet, briny and clean. When cooked, they release a flavourful broth that can become the base of soups, bee hoon, pasta, garlic butter sauces or Chinese wine dishes. This makes them excellent value because the shellfish contributes both meat and stock to the dish.

  • Sweet, juicy shellfish flavour
  • Fast cooking time
  • Suitable for soups, steaming and stir-fry
  • Good for family meals and sharing dishes
  • Pairs well with garlic, ginger, Chinese wine and chilli

How Much Manila Clams Should You Buy?

For most home meals, 1kg of Manila clams is suitable for 2 to 4 people depending on whether it is served as a main dish or side dish. For larger gatherings, customers often combine Manila clams with live crabs, lobsters or Soon Hock fish to create a full seafood meal.

If you are not sure how much to buy, tell us how many people you are cooking for and the style of dish you want. We can help suggest a practical quantity before you visit the stall.

Freshness Tips for Manila Clams

Fresh clams should be kept cool and cooked as soon as possible. Before cooking, rinse them properly and discard any clams that remain open and do not respond when tapped. For best results, avoid overcooking. Once the shells open, the clams are usually ready.
