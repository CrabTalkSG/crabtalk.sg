How to choose live crabs

Look at weight, shell condition, liveliness and the type of cooking you intend to do. For roe crabs, ask what is best on that day rather than buying blindly.

Best cooking styles

Many customers enjoy steaming roe crabs, chilli crab, ginger onion, black bean or simple home-cooked styles. The best method depends on crab type and freshness.
